Virgin Galactic is an American company that is part of the Virgin Group and plans to organize tourist suborbital space flights and launches of small artificial satellite. In the future, the company offers its customers and orbital flights.

Blue Origin

Blue Origin is an American private aerospace company headquartered in Kent, Washington. Created in 2000 by Amazon founder Jeff Bezos, and is located on his ranch, located 40 km north of Van Horn, Culberson County, Texas, USA.

NASA

The National Aeronautics and Space Administration is an agency within the federal government of the United States and reports directly to the President of the United States. NASA's mission is to pioneer the future in space exploration, aeronautics research.
